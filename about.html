Our Mission

We are a small family-owned company dedicated to providing fresh, delicious, and healthy fruit dishes to our customers. Our company was founded in 2000 by a group of passionate foodies who wanted to share their love of fruit with the world. We source all of our fruit from local farms and markets, and we pride ourselves on using only the highest quality ingredients in our dishes.

We are committed to promoting a healthy environment throughout the community. In addition to supplying a comprehensive range of organic foods and natural items, we also run our company in an honest and considerate manner, and we make charitable contributions to non-profit organizations that are struggling to make ends meet.

Our Story

We are a family-owned business that has been operating for over 10 years. Our passion for fresh, natural ingredients shines through in every bottle of juice we produce.

We have been Carlsbad's go-to destination for locally produced, organic, and bulk goods ever since we opened our doors more than four decades ago as Mr. Natural's Bountiful Food Store. Our name has been shortened recently. Additionally, the store is quite a bit larger. But we will continue to work toward the same goal.

The BOUNTIFUL is dedicated to supporting a healthy community. We provide a wide selection of organic food and natural products, conduct our business in an ethical and respectful manner and donate to organizations in need.
